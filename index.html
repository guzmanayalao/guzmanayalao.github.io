Who am I?

< Who am I? />

I grew up on the south side of Chicago. As a low-income youth seeking a better life for my family, I taught myself basic design and web development principles. At age 15, I hustled my way into the web development and graphic design worlds as a freelancer. 7 years later, I'm doing work for big-name companies like Comedy Central and finishing up my Computer Science and Design degrees. With so much riding on my success, I'm extremely motivated and determined to make sure I don't fail my clients.
